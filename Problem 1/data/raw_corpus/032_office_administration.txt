Welcome to Office of Administration The Office of Administration at IIT Jodhpur ensures the efficient functioning of the Institute’s administrative ecosystem through its various divisions/activities directly or with the help of Professor In-Charges (PIC’s) given as follows: Establishment (Non-Faculty) - Managing service matters related to non-teaching staff. Non-Faculty Recruitment – Handling end-to-end recruitment processes for non-teaching positions. Health Center – Ensuring quality healthcare services for the campus community. Campus Security and Transport – Overseeing campus safety and transport logistics. Logistics and Facilities – Managing day-to-day campus services, infrastructure maintenance, and operational support. Green Cell – Driving sustainability initiatives and environmental stewardship on campus. Visitor’s Hostels and Allied Services – Coordinating hospitality services for guests, visitors, and campus events. Internal Audit – Ensuring internal controls, compliance, and financial accountability. Store and Purchase – Managing procurement processes and inventory control for efficient resource utilization. Administrative Manpower Hiring for the Project. The office functions with the aim of ensuring transparency in the process, efficiency, accountability, and excellence. Working closely with different offices/sections, the office aims to deliver high-quality and timely services. It also frames and implements policies to foster a responsive culture to support institutional goals in education, development, and the well-being of the campus community. Dean Of Administration Prof. Shree Prakash Tiwari Email : doad@iitj.ac.in Phone: 0291 280 1032 Prof. Shree Prakash Tiwari assumed the office as the first Dean of Administration (DOAD) of IIT Jodhpur on 15th June 2024. DOAD position was created in 2024 as part of administrative reforms for efficient functioning. Prof. Tiwari is currently Professor in Department of Electrical Engineering. Prior to joining IIT Jodhpur as a faculty member in 2011, Prof. Tiwari had worked as a Postdoctoral Fellow in School of ECE at Georgia Institute of Technology, Atlanta, USA, for 3 years (2008-2011). He had done his Ph.D. from EE Department, IIT Bombay from 2003 to 2008, during which he had also worked at NTU Singapore for about 7 months. Prof. Tiwari is a Senior Member of IEEE and serves as selected member of technical committees on “Flexible Electronics and Displays” and “Electronic Materials” of IEEE Electron Devices Society. Prof. Tiwari was Visvesvaraya Young Faculty Research Fellow of Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology, Government of India, for 2018-2023. He was part of Indian Delegate to attend Indo-German Frontiers of Engineering Symposium in Bremen, Germany (2022) organized by Alexander von Humboldt Foundation and Department of Science and Technology, followed by CONNECT Follow-up Programme Award by Humboldt Foundation and IGSTC-CONNECT Plus Award to visit and work in Germany in 2023. At IIT Jodhpur, he leads the Flexible Large Area Microelectronics (FLAME) Research Group, with focus towards biodegradable electronics. He was recently conferred with “Institute Award for Research Excellence-2024, Senior Researcher Award (Engineering)” from IIT Jodhpur. He has co-authored over 170 research articles, including more than 85 in journals of high repute with many in IEEE, ACS, and Wiley Journals.
