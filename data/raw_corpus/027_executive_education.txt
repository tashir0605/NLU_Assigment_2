Welcome To The Office of Executive Education: A Transformative Odyssey for Visionary Trailblazers Pioneering Learning Frontiers Dynamic, forward-thinking programs meticulously crafted to empower professionals with the acumen and foresight to spearhead innovation. Helmed by IIT Jodhpur’s esteemed faculty and industry luminaries, our courses seamlessly weave groundbreaking research with practical applications, honing critical thinking and strategic mastery. Comprehensive Empowerment Tailored for ambitious professionals, our executive education offerings present an adaptable learning ecosystem—spanning in-person, online, and hybrid modalities. Participants thrive in an intellectually stimulating milieu, fortified by seamless administrative facilitation and unparalleled access to cutting-edge resources, ensuring a profoundly enriching and frictionless educational voyage. Enduring Alliances Transcending conventional academia, our programs foster deep-rooted professional synergies. Enrolees become part of an illustrious nexus of industry stalwarts, visionaries, and policymakers, paving the way for transformative collaborations and unparalleled career avenues. Purposeful Heterogeneity We convene a confluence of professionals hailing from myriad sectors, regions, and disciplines, catalysing a rich tapestry of intellectual exchange. This cultivated diversity fortifies leadership prowess, augments cross-sector problem-solving, and equips participants to adeptly navigate the labyrinth of contemporary global challenges. Vision To make IIT Jodhpur a leading Indian Institute offering world-class Executive Education for working professionals and other aspirants. Mission To provide well curated programmes in line with industry requirements. Provide opportunity to learners via both offline and online modes. Develop partnership with online Edu service providers (the best in the business). Draw industry’s attention to IITJ’s academic activities. Provide up-to-date insights and knowledge to participants. Gain access to ongoing and upcoming industry practices. Industry connect and participate in industry meet. Generate Revenue streams. Executive Programmes Structure at a glance: Executive M.Tech Programs: 60 credits (1 credit = 13 contact hours). Executive Postgraduate Diploma: 30 credits. Executive Postgraduate Certificate: 12-15 credits. Executive MBA Programs: 72 credits. Executive Undergraduate Programs: Year 1: Certification (40 credits), Year 2: Diploma (40 credits), Year 3: BSc Degree (40 credits), Year 4: BS Degree (40 credits). The Senate of the Institute has formulated a set of guidelines and rules to ensure high standard of performance as well as for smooth functioning of the academic programmes. Within this broad set of guidelines, subject to the approval of the Senate, different programmes can include additional academic requirements as deemed necessary for that programme. These academic programmes are continuously monitored by the Senate and make appropriate modifications/improvements as and when necessary.
